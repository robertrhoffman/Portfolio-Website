About Me

A native Floridian, I moved to Philadelphia for graduate school in 2009. I earned my PhD in philosophy in 2015. After several years teaching in higher education, I decided to apply my knowledge and skills to a new career in software development.

Coming from my unique background, I am fascinated by the way that language and concepts influence one another. Learning how to express concepts through programming languages has only furthered my interest. My goals as a programmer include:

  1. Write clear, effective code that follows industry best practices and satisfies client needs.
  2. Develop collaborative learning and working relationships with my peers, so that I may
    • be an effective, valued team member
    • continue to grow and improve throughout my career
  3. Pursue a career that enables me to promote positive change in the world around me.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, writing, hiking with my wife, and playing with my cats.
